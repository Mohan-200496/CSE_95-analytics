Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on Punjab Rozgar Portal?

To create an account, click on "Register" in the top navigation, choose your user type (Job Seeker or Employer), fill in the required information, and verify your email address.

How can I search for jobs?

Use the job search feature on our homepage or browse jobs page. You can filter by location, job category, experience level, and employment type to find relevant opportunities.

How do employers post job openings?

Employers need to register and verify their account, then use the "Post a Job" feature from their dashboard. All job postings are reviewed by our team before going live.

Is the portal free to use?

Yes, Punjab Rozgar Portal is completely free for both job seekers and employers. It's a government initiative to promote employment in Punjab.

What should I do if I forgot my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.
